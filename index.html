About Me

Hey everyone! I'm ARD0NIZ, a passionate bot developer who has been working on Discord bots since December 2018. Over the years, I've built 5 bots, gaining experience and constantly evolving along the way.

Some of my earlier projects are no longer active, as I've shifted my focus to a new bot niche and continued developing new ideas. In 2021, I also set up a test server for developers, a place to experiment with bots, learn, and exchange knowledge.

Like many projects, activity slowed down during the pandemic, but now I'm motivated to revive the community and build something new. Right now, I'm actively looking for members who want to learn, help out, or grow together.

If you're wondering what I can help with: I work with HTML, CSS, BDFD, Aoi.js, and ForgeScript. Whether you're looking for coding help, want to get into bot development, or are simply searching for a place to connect with others, you're very welcome to join.
